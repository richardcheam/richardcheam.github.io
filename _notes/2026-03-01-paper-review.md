## 10-Minute Pass

- Problem statement in one sentence.
- Core method in one sentence.
- Key result in one sentence.

## Deep Pass

- What assumptions are hidden?
- What data or constraints limit transferability?
- What part is reusable in my projects?

## Action Block

- One experiment to reproduce.
- One metric to validate.
- One risk if this is applied in production.
